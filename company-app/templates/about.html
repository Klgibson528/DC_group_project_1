About PediPaws

At PediPaws, discover professional grooming services performed by experienced groomers who love animals! We are passionate about positive training methods with rewards, never harsh words or punishment. With a certified groomer on staff, we do everything to provide your pet with a fantastic spa day. No matter the age, size or breed of your four legged friend are happy to help. Your furry friend will be greeted by name and we will ensure that he or she is comfortable and happy through the entire process. We will treat your pet with love and respect and as if he or she were our own.
